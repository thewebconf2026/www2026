Conference Venue

Conference activities will be held in Dubai Festival City, a large, self-contained waterfront community on Dubai Creek featuring the major Dubai Festival City Mall, luxury hotels, Golf Club, entertainment, and waterfront dining.

InterContinental Dubai Festival City

Address: Al Kheeran – Dubai Festival City – Dubai – United Arab Emirates
Phone: +971 4 701 1111

The conference will take place at the InterContinental Dubai Festival City by IHG, a major international conference hotel located along Dubai Creek. The venue offers modern conference infrastructure, including large plenary spaces, multiple breakout rooms, and full audiovisual support, and has extensive experience hosting large academic and industrial events. The hotel and conference center is part of Dubai Festival City, a mixed-use district that includes hotels, restaurants, and shopping areas within walking distance. It is located approximately 10 minutes from Dubai International Airport and is easily accessible by car and taxi from all parts of the city.

About Dubai

Dubai is a major international city strategically located on the southeastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, along the Arabian Gulf. Its geographic position at the crossroads of Europe, Asia, and Africa makes it a natural global meeting point. Dubai International Airport is among the world’s busiest, offering direct connections to most major cities worldwide and enabling convenient travel for international conference participants.

The city combines a rich cultural heritage with modern urban development and world-class infrastructure. Alongside historic districts, museums, and traditional markets, Dubai features contemporary architecture, waterfront areas, and a diverse culinary scene. Its safety, accessibility, and mature hospitality ecosystem make it a practical and welcoming destination for large international academic and professional conferences.

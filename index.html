Welcome to The Web Conference 2026

Since the invention of the World Wide Web in 1989, The Web Conference (formerly known as International World Wide Web Conference, abbreviated as WWW) is a yearly international academic conference on the topic of the future direction of the World Wide Web. This conference has been the premier venue to present and discuss progress in research, development, standards, and applications of the topics related to the Web.

The 2026 ACM Web Conference will offer a high-quality program made of research sessions, posters and demonstrations, a PhD symposium for junior scholars, workshops, tutorials, a developers track for practitioners, as well as thought-provoking keynote speakers, panels, special tracks, and colocated special days.

Venue

The Web Conference 2026 will be held in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Dubai is a global hub for business, tourism, and innovation, making it an ideal location for The Web Conference.

The specific venue details will be announced closer to the conference date. Dubai offers world-class conference facilities with state-of-the-art technology and amenities.

Dubai is easily accessible from around the world, with direct flights to over 240 destinations through Dubai International Airport (DXB), one of the world's busiest international airports.

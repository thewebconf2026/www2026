Special Tracks

The Web Conference 2026 features several special tracks that focus on specific areas of web research and development. These tracks provide dedicated venues for specialized topics that are central to the web community.

History of the Web

Exploring the evolution, milestones, and lessons learned from the development of the World Wide Web. This track examines historical perspectives, foundational technologies, and the people who shaped the web as we know it today.

About Special Tracks

Special tracks at The Web Conference provide focused venues for emerging and specialized areas of web research. These tracks maintain the same high standards of peer review as the main conference tracks while allowing for deeper exploration of specific themes and communities.

Papers accepted to special tracks will be published in the main conference proceedings and presented during dedicated sessions at the conference.

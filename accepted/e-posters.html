e-posters

Information for authors preparing e-posters for poster sessions at The ACM Web Conference 2026.

Preparation

Authors of papers assigned to one of the five Poster sessions, as indicated in the Conference Full Schedule, may begin preparing their e-posters using this template.

Please note that the number, labels, and positions of the template boxes may be modified as desired, provided that the specified font types and sizes are maintained.

Submission

The designated registered author will receive submission instructions and an upload link directly from our PCO, MCI, shortly. Please check your spam folder for email from @wearemci.com.

Please submit your e-poster and supplementary material by 25 June for inclusion on the virtual platform.

Presentation Requirement

Please note that failure to present your poster virtually during your designated session may result in your paper being excluded from the Proceedings.
